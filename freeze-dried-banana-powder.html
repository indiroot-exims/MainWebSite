Freeze-Dried Banana Powder

Naturally sweet, potassium-rich, and processed without heat to preserve color, flavor and nutrition.

Request a Quote

Freeze-dried banana powder is made from ripe bananas, flash-frozen and dried under vacuum to lock in their natural sweetness, aroma and potassium content — without the heat degradation of conventional drying methods. The result is a fine, cream-colored powder ideal for smoothie mixes, infant food, bakery formulations and functional snacks.
