Why Spanish Companies Source Banana Powder from IndiRoot Exims

Spain is one of Europe's largest food and beverage markets, with a well-established food manufacturing sector and a fast-growing nutraceutical and sports nutrition industry centred around Barcelona and Madrid. Spanish food manufacturers increasingly specify clean-label, plant-based functional ingredients like banana powder for both the domestic EU market and for export-oriented product lines sold into Latin America — a unique dual-market advantage that Spanish buyers hold over most other European food companies.

IndiRoot Exims supplies bulk banana powder directly from India to Spanish food manufacturers, nutraceutical brands, dairy producers, beverage companies, and ingredient distributors.
