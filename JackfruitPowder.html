JackFruit Powder

Jackfruit powder is a natural food product made by drying and grinding the edible flesh of the jackfruit into a fine powder. It preserves the flavor, nutrients, and health benefits of jackfruit while offering convenience and a longer shelf life.

Color: Off White

Drying Method: Spray Dried

JackFruitSeed Powder

Jackfruit seed powder is made by drying and grinding the seeds of jackfruit (Artocarpus heterophyllus). While often discarded, jackfruit seeds are nutrient-rich and possess several health-promoting properties.

Dehydrated JackFruit Powder

Dehydrated Jackfruit Powder is made by removing moisture from ripe jackfruit pulp using controlled heat drying (typically hot air drying or vacuum drying), and then grinding it into a fine powder.

Shelf Life: 12 Months
