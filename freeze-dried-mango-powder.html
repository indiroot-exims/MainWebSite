Freeze-Dried Mango Powder

Vibrant color, tropical aroma, and a rich source of Vitamin A & C — preserved through low-temperature vacuum drying.

Request a Quote

Freeze-dried mango powder is made from ripe mangoes, flash-frozen and dried under vacuum to retain their natural golden color, tropical aroma and Vitamin A & C content — without the flavor loss typical of heat-based drying. Ideal for beverage mixes, bakery, confectionery and functional food applications.
