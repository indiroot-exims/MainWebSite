1. Preserving Nutrients through Shade-Drying

While sun-drying is common in bulk production, it often leads to oxidation, turning powder brownish and destroying heat-sensitive antioxidants. The IndiRoot Process utilizes a controlled Shade-Drying method. By avoiding direct UV exposure, we preserve the vibrant green color and the complex nutritional profile (Vitamins A & C) that premium brands demand, resulting in a 24-month shelf life.

2. The "Zero-Detection" Standard (BLQ)

We subject our Moringa Leaf Powder to rigorous testing at NABL-accredited facilities such as Tentamus Labs. Our quality benchmark is the BLQ (Below Limit of Quantification) standard. We monitor over 200+ multi-residue pesticides, ensuring compliance with EU Regulation (EC) 396/2005 and US EPA tolerance levels to provide a "Green Channel" for your shipments through international customs.

3. Heavy Metal & Microbial Integrity

Soil quality is as important as the plant itself. We proactively monitor heavy metal profiles (Lead, Arsenic, Cadmium) to ensure our Moringa remains a safe ingredient for supplements and smoothies. Our gentle processing environment is managed to ensure a Pathogen-Free Guarantee, with zero presence of Salmonella or E. coli.
