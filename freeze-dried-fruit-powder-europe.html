What to Expect Shipping to Europe

A few things specific to EU-bound orders, based on how our current buyers typically work with us.

Regulatory Status

As a traditionally consumed dried-fruit product made by removing water rather than by novel processing, freeze-dried fruit powder is not typically expected to fall under EU Novel Food regulation — but we'd always recommend confirming this with your own regulatory counsel for your specific end-use before import.

Testing & Compliance

Full microbiology, pesticide residue (MRL) and heavy metal test reports are provided per lot, to support your own EU food safety documentation and due diligence.

Shipping & Incoterms

Typically shipped FOB from Nhava Sheva or Mundra port, with CIF available on request. Sea freight transit to major EU ports is generally in the 20–28 day range depending on destination and season — we'll confirm current transit estimates at the time of quotation.

Private Label

Bulk and private-label packaging options are available for European retail, foodservice, and nutraceutical brands.
