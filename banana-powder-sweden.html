Why Swedish Companies Source Banana Powder from IndiRoot Exims

Sweden has one of Europe's strongest plant-based food cultures — home to globally recognised brands in oat-based dairy alternatives, organic food retail, and functional nutrition. Swedish consumers consistently rank among the highest in Europe for organic food consumption per capita, and Swedish food manufacturers are early adopters of clean-label, traceable, and sustainably sourced plant-based ingredients. Banana powder — particularly organic and freeze dried variants — fits directly into this demand landscape.

IndiRoot Exims supplies bulk banana powder directly from India to Swedish food manufacturers, nutraceutical brands, organic food companies, dairy and plant-based beverage producers, and ingredient distributors. Sweden's Port of Gothenburg is Scandinavia's largest container port and our primary destination for Swedish shipments, with typical sea transit of 20–25 days from Indian ports (Mundra, Nhava Sheva) on FOB and CIF terms. Swedish-based ingredient distributors also frequently re-supply Norway, Finland, and Denmark from Gothenburg, and we support larger Nordic-region bulk orders on request.

Our Organic Banana Powder is supplied with EU Organic certification documentation, which forms the basis for Sweden's KRAV certification — Sweden's own stricter organic standard. All shipment documentation is aligned with EU food import law including pesticide MRL compliance under Regulation (EC) 396/2005.

Sweden has a highly developed sports nutrition and gut health supplement market, and raw green banana powder is increasingly specified as a plant-based RS2 source for Swedish supplement brands targeting the domestic Nordic market and for export to EU health food retailers.
