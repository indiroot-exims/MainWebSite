Business Terms & Order Process

We are a merchant exporter specializing in organic food powders. All sales are conducted through direct business inquiry.

How to Order

  1. Contact us for product availability and pricing
  3. Receive quotation based on your requirements
  4. Confirm order via email / written agreement
  5. Process payment as per agreed terms

Our Commitment

  • ✅ FSSAI certified products
  • ✅ Lab test certificates available
  • ✅ Quality guarantee as per agreed specifications
  • ✅ Export documentation support

Refund/Replacement Eligibility:

  • Refunds or replacements will only be processed if the product is found to be defective, damaged during transit, or incorrect as per your order.
  • Customers must notify us within 7 days of delivery with relevant proof (photos/videos).

Non-Refundable Conditions:

  • Products once opened, used, or tampered with cannot be refunded due to food safety and hygiene concerns.
  • Minor variations in color, texture, or aroma of powders are natural in organic products and do not qualify as defects.
  • Refunds will not be issued for bulk orders exported unless there is a clear quality issue verified by lab reports or agreed specifications.

Process:

  • Contact us at info@indirootexims.com with your order details and issue.
  • Our team will verify and confirm eligibility.
  • If approved, refund/replacement will be processed as per the agreed business terms.

For bulk inquiries and export orders, please contact us directly.
