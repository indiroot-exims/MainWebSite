What to Expect Shipping to the USA

A few things specific to US-bound orders, based on how our current buyers typically work with us.

Labeling & Documentation

We provide COA, SDS and ingredient statements formatted to support your own FDA nutrition labeling and allergen declaration process. Final label compliance remains the importer's responsibility, and we're happy to work with your regulatory team on the specifics.

Shipping & Incoterms

Typically shipped FOB from Nhava Sheva or Mundra port, with CIF available on request. Sea freight transit to US coasts is generally in the 30–40 day range depending on port and season — we'll confirm current transit estimates at the time of quotation.

Testing & Compliance

Pesticide residue and heavy metal test reports are available per lot, useful for your own FDA prior-notice and import compliance documentation.

Private Label

Bulk and private-label packaging options are available for US retail, DTC, and nutraceutical brands looking to launch under their own label.
