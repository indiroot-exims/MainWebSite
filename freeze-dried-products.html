How It's Made

The Freeze-Drying Process

Every batch follows the same disciplined sequence, from raw material to finished product.

01

Harvest

Sourced at peak ripeness/freshness for maximum natural flavor and color.

02

Flash Freezing

Rapidly frozen to lock in structure before moisture is removed.

03

Vacuum Drying

Frozen water sublimates directly to vapor under vacuum — no heat damage.

04

Milling / Cutting

Milled to powder or cut to piece size depending on the product.

05

QC & Packing

Quality-checked and packed in food-grade, moisture-proof packaging.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does freeze-dried cost more than spray-dried or hot-air dried powder?

Freeze-drying is slower and more energy-intensive, but preserves color, flavor and nutrients far better than heat-based drying — reflected in both the process and the premium end-use it's suited for.
