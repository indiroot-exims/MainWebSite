3. What is the MOQ for bulk moringa powder export to Europe?

Our standard Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) is 500 kg per shipment for all variants (Organic, Natural, and Finely Milled).

5. Which European countries do you regularly export to?

We ship directly to port hubs and distributors across Germany, France, the UK, the Netherlands, Italy, Spain, Poland, Belgium, Sweden, Denmark, and Austria.

6. What wholesale packaging frameworks are available?

Our baseline bulk configuration is a 20-25 kg dual-walled, food-grade kraft paper bag complete with internal protective poly-liners. Custom sizing or specialized configurations are available for multi-ton contracts.

7. Is Indian moringa powder subject to EU Novel Food rules?

Moringa oleifera leaves have an established history of consumption within the European Union before 1997 and are widely accepted in food and dietary supplements. We provide all essential analytical data to satisfy local regulatory parameters.
