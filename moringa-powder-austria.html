Frequently Asked Questions — Austria Custom Clearances

1. Does your Moringa Powder comply with AGES regulatory frameworks in Austria?

Yes. Our export batches undergo systematic laboratory testing validating strict compliance with the Austrian Agency for Health and Food Safety (AGES) guidelines. We provide fully authenticated independent COAs detailing microbiological thresholds and EU heavy metal conformity.

2. How is bulk cargo routed to landlocked Austria from India?

As Austria is a landlocked market, we optimize shipping pipelines via major European maritime gateways, primarily the ports of Rotterdam or Hamburg. From these entry terminals, cargo is seamlessly routed to your processing centers in Vienna, Graz, or Linz via secure, temperature-controlled intermodal block trains or road freight.

3. Is your export-grade moringa powder treated with irradiation or ethylene oxide?

No. Our Moringa powder is 100% natural and processed using clean, low-temperature dehydration. We strictly do not apply irradiation, ETO (Ethylene Oxide), or chemical fumigants, making it fully compliant with organic and natural supplement standards in Scandinavia.

4. What are your shipping configurations and container options for Austrian buyers?

We ship bulk orders starting at a minimum order quantity (MOQ) of 500 kg. For large-scale manufacturing accounts across Austria, we provide 20ft and 40ft Full Container Loads (FCL) delivered on clear sea and land intermodal delivery terms.

5. What type of mesh sizes do you process for Austrian tea blending and supplement labs?

We offer fine-milled profiles from 80-120 mesh for functional herbal teas and up to a micro-milled 200 mesh for advanced nutritional tableting, ensuring seamless processing on high-speed Austrian manufacturing machinery.
