Why Dutch Companies Source Banana Powder from IndiRoot Exims

The Netherlands is Europe's largest food and ingredient import and re-distribution hub. Rotterdam — the continent's biggest container port — handles a significant share of all food ingredient imports entering the EU, and Dutch ingredient distributors, traders, and food manufacturers are among the most active buyers of bulk plant-based powders for both domestic use and onward distribution across Germany, Belgium, France, Scandinavia, and Eastern Europe.

IndiRoot Exims ships bulk banana powder directly from Indian ports (Mundra, Nhava Sheva) to Rotterdam on FOB and CIF terms, with typical sea transit of 18–22 days. The Netherlands' strategic position as the EU's primary ingredient gateway makes it an ideal sourcing point for distributors supplying multiple European markets from a single import — and we support larger consolidated bulk orders for re-distribution across the EU on request.
