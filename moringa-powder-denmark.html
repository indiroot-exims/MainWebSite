Frequently Asked Questions — Denmark Custom Clearances

1. Does your Moringa Powder comply with Fødevarestyrelsen regulatory frameworks?

Yes. Our export batches undergo systematic laboratory testing validating strict compliance with the Danish Veterinary and Food Administration (Fødevarestyrelsen) standards. We provide fully authenticated independent COAs detailing microbiological thresholds and EU heavy metal conformity.

2. How do you verify your bulk Moringa powder is compliant with EU pesticide regulations?

Every shipment bound for Denmark is audited by accredited third-party laboratories via liquid and gas chromatography mass spectrometry (LC-MS/MS & GC-MS/MS). We screen against more than 500 individual chemical residues to guarantee compliance with Regulation (EC) No 396/2005.

3. Is your export-grade moringa powder treated with irradiation or ethylene oxide?

No. Our Moringa powder is 100% natural and processed using clean, low-temperature dehydration. We strictly do not apply irradiation, ETO (Ethylene Oxide), or chemical fumigants, making it fully compliant with organic and natural supplement standards in Scandinavia.

4. What are your shipping configurations and container options for Copenhagen and Aarhus?

We ship bulk orders starting at a minimum order quantity (MOQ) of 500 kg. For large-scale manufacturing accounts across Denmark, we provide 20ft and 40ft Full Container Loads (FCL) on clear CIF Copenhagen or CIF Aarhus sea freight delivery terms.

5. What type of mesh sizes do you process for precision supplement tableting?

We provide premium micro-milled arrays stretching up to 120-200 mesh. This fine-mesh profiling ensures rapid dispersibility in functional drink mixes and seamless capsule filling with zero flowability issues on industrial machinery.
