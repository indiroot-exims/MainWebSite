Why Italian Companies Source Banana Powder from IndiRoot Exims

Italy is one of Europe's largest food manufacturing nations — home to globally recognised industries in confectionery, dairy, pasta, baby food, and nutraceuticals. Brands across these sectors are increasingly incorporating plant-based functional ingredients such as banana powder into new product lines responding to Italian consumer demand for natural, minimally processed, and functional food inputs.

IndiRoot Exims supplies bulk banana powder directly from India to Italian food manufacturers, nutraceutical brands, dairy and gelato flavouring companies, beverage producers, and ingredient distributors. Spray dried banana powder is particularly suited to Italy's large dairy and gelato flavouring sector, while freeze dried variants are increasingly specified by Italy's growing premium health food and sports nutrition market.

Italy has direct sea access via the Port of Genoa, Port of Trieste, and Port of Livorno — making logistics from Indian ports (Mundra, Nhava Sheva) efficient on FOB and CIF terms. All documentation is fully aligned with EU food import law including EU pesticide MRL compliance under Regulation (EC) 396/2005 and EU traceability requirements under Regulation (EC) 178/2002.
