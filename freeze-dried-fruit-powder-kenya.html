What to Expect Shipping to Kenya

A few things specific to Kenya and wider East Africa-bound orders, based on how our current buyers typically work with us.

KEBS & Conformity

We can support documentation aligned with Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS) requirements, including pre-export conformity assessment where applicable. Final conformity certification remains the importer's responsibility, and we're happy to work with your compliance team on specifics.

Shipping & Incoterms

Typically shipped FOB from Nhava Sheva or Mundra port to Mombasa. Sea freight transit is generally in the 12–18 day range depending on season and routing — we'll confirm current transit estimates at the time of quotation.

Regional Distribution

Many of our Kenyan buyers use Mombasa/Nairobi as a distribution point for onward supply into Tanzania, Uganda and Rwanda under East African Community (EAC) trade arrangements — we can package and label with that downstream distribution in mind.

Testing & Documentation

COA, SDS and pesticide/heavy-metal test reports are available per lot to support your import and regional distribution documentation.
